About this Site

Parents can use this app to find groups for their kids to walk to school in. To use this feature, parents must sign up, entering their child's name, address, and school. We'll check with the school to verify that this account is for a valid student. Once an account is created, parents can see already formed walking groups. Parents can either determine a meeting location or use the website's suggested optimal location.
